About BeerMe

BeerMe is a platform for beer enthusiasts to track and rate their beer experiences. Our mission is to help people discover new beers and breweries, and share their experiences with a community of fellow beer lovers.

Whether you're a casual beer drinker or a connoisseur, BeerMe offers features to enhance your beer journey. Keep track of the beers you've tried, rate them, and add notes about each one. You can also see what beers and breweries other users are enjoying.

We're constantly working to improve BeerMe and add new features. We welcome feedback and suggestions from our users.

Happy beer tasting!
